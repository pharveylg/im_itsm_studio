# OAS-KB-004 Operational Examples

## Purpose

Full, illustrative analyses that demonstrate the OAS methodologies applied end-to-end.
These are **fictional** but realistic; use them as models when performing real analyses.
Each follows the report templates in **OAS-KB-003** and the checklists in **OAS-KB-002**.

## Contents

| Example | Standard | Record |
|---------|----------|--------|
| `example-incident-analysis-INC0012345.md` | OAS-101 | Incident |
| `example-problem-analysis-PRB000789.md` | OAS-301 | Problem |
| `example-change-analysis-CHG001122.md` | OAS-401 | Change |

A Major Incident communications example (OAS-201) is intentionally omitted here to keep
these artefacts focused; the OAS-201 worked example in the standard can be extended the
same way.
